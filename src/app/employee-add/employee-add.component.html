New Employee Information

  • The First Name is required.
  • The First Name must be at least 2 characters long
  • The First Name must be at most 25 characters long.
  • The First Name must be letters Only.
  • The Last Name is required.
  • The Last Name must be at least 2 characters long
  • The Last Name must be at most 35 characters long.
  • The Last Name must be letters Only.
  • The Address is required.
  • The Address must be at least 10 characters long
  • The Address must be at most 50 characters long.
  • The Address must be letters, numbers, hyphens, apostrophes, or spaces.
  • The City is required.
  • The City must be at least 5 characters long
  • The City must be at most 50 characters long.
  • The City must be letters Only.
  • The State is required
  • The ZIP/Postal Code is required.
  • The ZIP/Postal Code must be at least 5 characters long
  • The ZIP/Postal Code must be at most 9 characters long.
  • The ZIP/Postal Code must be Numbers Only.
  • The Cell Phone is required.
  • The Cell Phone must be at least 10 characters long
  • The Cell Phone must be at most 10 characters long with No "-".
  • The Cell Phone must be Numbers Only.
  • The Home Phone is required.
  • The Home Phone must be at least 10 characters long
  • The Home Phone must be at most 10 characters long with No "-".
  • The Home Phone must be Numbers Only.
  • The Email is required.
  • The Email must be at least 10 characters long
  • The Email must be at most 50 characters long.
  • The Email must be a valid Email address.
